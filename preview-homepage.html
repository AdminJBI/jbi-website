Frequently Asked

Before your first visit.

The questions we hear most from patients considering consultation — answered clearly so you can focus on what matters. Don't see yours? Call us anytime.

Ask Us Directly →
01
How long is recovery from anterior hip replacement?
Most patients are walking with assistance the same day of surgery and off a walker within two to three weeks. Full recovery — including return to most activities — typically takes six to twelve weeks. The anterior approach is muscle-sparing, which is why recovery is significantly faster than traditional posterior techniques.
02
Do I need a referral to schedule a consultation?
Most insurance plans do not require a referral to see Dr. Srivastava, but some HMO plans do. Call our office with your insurance information and we will verify what's needed before your first visit.
03
Will surgery be recommended at my first visit?
Almost never. Our philosophy is conservative care first. Most joint and bone pain responds to physical therapy, targeted injections, or regenerative treatments. Surgery is recommended only when non-surgical options have been exhausted or won't address the underlying problem.
04
What is robotic knee surgery, and is it safer?
Robotic-assisted knee replacement uses real-time imaging and a surgeon-guided robotic arm to place the implant with sub-millimeter precision. Studies show better alignment, less soft-tissue damage, and longer implant life compared to traditional techniques. Dr. Srivastava is fellowship-trained in this approach.
05
Do you offer payment plans if I don't have insurance?
Yes. We partner with Care Credit for flexible financing and offer in-house payment plans for self-pay patients. Our billing team will provide transparent cost estimates before any procedure or imaging order — no surprise bills.
06
Which location should I visit?
Dr. Srivastava sees patients at all three locations — Bakersfield, Porterville, and Delano. Choose whichever is most convenient for you. Surgical procedures are performed at affiliated surgery centers; our office staff will coordinate all logistics.
